I was born and raised up in China, and I obtained my master degree in electrical engineering from Peking University. Then I started my Ph.D. research at imec in Belgium, and earned my Ph.D. degree from KULeuven in 2021.

My Ph.D. topic was focused on developing preventative mhealth and self-care solutions via passive monitoring of health behaviors. Firstly, I engineered a non-invasive monitoring system to monitor the moment-to-moment biopsychosocialenvironmental context of each user. The system was comprised of three wearable sensors, a cloud database, a website and a mobile phone app. Secondly, by fusing these data collected from all the sensors, we build up a model that was able to understand and adapt to user's phenotype and varying context. Finally, we could give dynamically personalized and temporally-precise suggestion/interventions to each individual, so that we can nudge people with unhealthy lifestyle to be healthy and stay healthy.

I am mostly interested in these topics: quantified-self, preventitative healthcare, machine learning, artificial intelligence, data analytics
